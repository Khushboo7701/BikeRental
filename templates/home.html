    Frequently Asked Questions

    Renting a bike should be easy, just like our FAQs.

    • How can I book my ride?

      Just login to RentIt, Select the bike which you want to book and checkout. Once your payment is processed, you get the complete details of the dealer with location link and contact number. You shall also get the contact details of your dedicated trip manager at the same time. Just collect your bike and ride off. It's super easy, isn't it?

    • Will I get a refund if I cancel the booking?

      Upon booking a bike with any of our dealers, they reserve the bike for the customer. In case of cancellation unnecessary inconvenience is caused to the dealer. To account for the potential financial loss, in case of cancellation before 24 hours of the Bike pick up time, 80% of the amount paid is refunded. However, if the cancellation is done within 24 hours of the pick up time no refund can be availed.

    • Can the date of a reservation be changed?

      No, since the bikes have been booked and reserved by our Dealers. You can cancel your current booking and then book a bike for a later date, however a cancellation fee will apply.

    • What if I meet with an accident during the ride?

      We treat accidents and injuries during bike rides very seriously. If you are in such a situation, you should first try to get any immediate in person help possible. Followed by this call the vendor immediately to inform him of the mishap. We advise you to inform us of the incident as soon as possible as well.

    • I want to make a booking for one month. Shall I return the bike daily?

      No, you just need to return it at the drop date/ time, which you select on the website.
